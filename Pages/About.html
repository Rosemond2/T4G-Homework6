At Korena Claudia, We Connect Africa's Bounty to the World.

Here at Korena Claudia, we're passionate about bringing the rich tapestry of Africa's raw materials to the global market.

We source ethically and sustainably from across the continent, working directly with farmers and cooperatives to ensure fair trade practices and empower local communities.

Our commitment extends beyond just sourcing. We prioritize preserving traditional harvesting methods and minimizing environmental impact.

From sun-dried Ethiopian coffee beans to vibrant Ghanaian shea butter, each product embodies the unique character of its origin.

Avocado

It's widely cultivated in Africa and enjoys global popularity for its creamy flesh, large single seed, and versatility in various dishes.

African Cashew

Africa boasts a growing industry of delicious roasted and flavored cashews, offering a taste of sunshine and supporting local communities.

Gari

Gari, the beloved Ghanaian staple food made from cassava dough, is a versatile and nutritious treasure.

Shea Butter

Shea butter is extracted from the nuts of the African shea tree. It's rich in vitamins and is commonly used in cosmetics and moisturizers.

African Coffee

African coffee is known for its rich flavors and aromas. Each region offers a unique taste profile, making it a favorite among coffee enthusiasts.
